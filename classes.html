Yoga Classes

Gentle Hatha Yoga
A 60 minutes class of poses and slow movement that focuses on asana (proper alignment and posture), pranayama (breath work), and guidedmeditation to foster your mind and bod connection. This class is intended for beginners and anyone wishing a grounded foundation in the practice of yoga.
Vinyasa Yoga
A 60 minute class that focuses on breath-synchronized movement — you will inhale and exhale as you flow energetically through yoga poses. While intended for intermediate to advanced students, beginners are welcome to join in this class.
Restorative Yoga
A 90 minute class that features very slow movement and long poses. Resotrative yoga is useful in relieving stress and fostering a sense of well-being. This calming, restorative experience is suitable for studentsof any level of experience.
